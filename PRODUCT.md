# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Engineers who want to move into AI startups, actively hunting for the right founder to join. They're technically literate, time-poor, and value signal over noise. They use Scout to filter dozens of startups to the 3–5 founders worth reaching out to, then use the generated message as a launch pad.

## Product Purpose

Scout searches startups via the CrustData API, uses Claude to rank founders by fit with the user's role interest, and generates personalized outreach messages. Success means the user ends a session with a short list of qualified founders and a ready-to-send message for each — zero wasted outreach.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, precise, efficient. Decisions are clear, information is surfaced without clutter, and every action moves the user closer to a sent message. No hedging, no decorative noise.

## Anti-references

- LinkedIn: corporate chrome, dense sidebar nav, heavy blue brand
- Generic SaaS (Notion/Linear clones): muted warm neutrals + indigo CTAs, eyebrow-heavy section scaffolding
- Startup landing pages: hero animations, big brand claims, gradient text, metric grids

## Design Principles

1. **Signal before structure.** Fit score and founder name are what matter. Surface them first, and keep everything else subordinate.
2. **Zero wasted steps.** Every screen state has one clear next action. No dead ends, no ambiguous flows.
3. **Earned decoration.** Visual interest comes from typography and spacing rhythm, not color or ornamentation.
4. **Honest density.** Show the right amount of information for the task — not sparse (feels toy-like), not crowded (feels overwhelming).
5. **Speed as a feature.** Interactions must feel instant. Progressive feedback (SSE streaming) is a first-class UX concern.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Good color contrast (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large text). Keyboard-navigable flows. Screen-reader-compatible component structure.
